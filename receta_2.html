So Mexican So delicious

For me this is the best way to get close to Mexican traditional food. A very traditional and versatil dish

INGREDIENTS

  1. Totopos (fried tortilla on traingule or squere shape)
  2. Special Salsa
  3. Cheese
  4. Onion
  5. Cream

DIRECTIONS

1- Salsa
Boil the salsa for around 30 min
2- Altogether
Get the totopos into the sauce
Put everything else there
Add the chees, onion and cream over the Chilaquiles
